The NOIRIA Story

Founded in 2025 by some Unkown students from IIM Bangalore , NOIRIA represents the pinnacle of chocolate craftsmanship. Each creation is the result of hundreds of hours of research, testing, and refinement.

We source only the rarest cocoa beans from remote plantations, working directly with farmers who share our commitment to ethical and sustainable practices. Our chocolates contain no artificial additives or preservatives - just pure, unadulterated flavor.

Every NOIRIA piece is handcrafted in our atelier in Paris, using techniques perfected over generations. We combine traditional methods with innovative approaches to create chocolates that are both timeless and contemporary.
